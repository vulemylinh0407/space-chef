COMING SOON

SPACE CHEFS
FROM SPACE

STORYLINE

A troupe of alien chefs travel the galaxy, cooking food for those who will have them. The only problem is they don't know how to cook!

Unhappy with their inability to cook, the alien's manager tells them to retrieve a secret recipe book full of the best recipes located on a planet known as Earth.

More specifically, the secret recipe book is located on a small farm and guarded by its two residents, file names: G-Ma and G-Pa. They are considered to be no threat to the mission.

To limit escape, the aliens create a plan to abduct the human's home and move it in the middle of their cornfield. Little did the alien's know, someone had been listening to them the whole time and they were ready for their arrival.
